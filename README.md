# Elliot's Archive

This repository is a living archive of everything I have built, written, and thought about — spanning engineering and sales funnels, academic research and long-form writing, mathematics and design. The common thread is not the subject matter. It is a way of thinking: rigorous, curious, and drawn to problems that sit at the edges of disciplines.

I am currently a PhD researcher in boiling solver model development in collaboration with Rolls-Royce PLC, and a mathematics and physics tutor whose students go on to read at Oxford, Cambridge and Imperial. Before that, I spent several years in the commercial world — building websites, writing copy, and designing the sales systems that turn an audience into a business. I approached all of it the same way: as an engineer.
